How It Works

From first inquiry
to final song.

CuePoint covers every stage of every event so nothing falls through the cracks and nothing gets lost in your head.

01 · Capture
Inquiry comes in
Your booking link captures every inquiry with package selection, event details, and contact info. Lands directly in your Leads pipeline with full context.
02 · Convert
One click to booking
Convert a lead to a booking in a single action. Contract goes out for e-signature, invoice is created, and the event appears on your calendar · all at once.
03 · Plan
Build the experience
Build the timeline, select music for every moment, send questionnaires, and brief the client through their private portal. Everything connected to the same event.
04 · Perform
Show up ready
Every detail is in your pocket. Client preferences, special songs, timeline, venue notes. You walk in prepared, not scrambling.
